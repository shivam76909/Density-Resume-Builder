Frequently Asked Questions

Is Density Resume Builder really free?

Yes, 100%. We do not have any premium tiers, hidden charges, or watermarks. Our mission is to make professional resume building accessible to every student and job seeker.

What is an ATS-friendly resume?

Most large companies use Applicant Tracking Systems to scan resumes for keywords. An ATS-friendly resume uses standard headings and simple formatting that these systems can easily parse, ensuring your CV reaches a human recruiter.

Is my data safe with Density?

Yes. Density is a client-side tool. This means your data is never uploaded to our servers. It stays in your browser's local memory until you clear it or download your PDF.

Do I need to create an account?

No. You can start building your resume immediately without providing any personal email or creating a password.
