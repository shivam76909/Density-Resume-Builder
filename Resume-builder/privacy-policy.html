Privacy Policy

At Density, we prioritize your data security. This policy outlines how we handle your professional journey's information.

Updated: April 2026 • Reading time: 6 min

1. Personal Information We Collect

When you use Density Resume Builder, we collect data essential for document generation. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Full name, professional title, and contact details.
  • Professional History: Employment dates, company names, and job responsibilities.
  • Educational Data: Degrees, certifications, and academic institutions.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device identifiers collected via log files.

2. How We Process Your Data

Density is a human-centric platform. We do not use automated algorithms or AI to interpret your data. We use it to:

  • Populate our high-precision, ATS-friendly templates.
  • Improve platform performance and fix technical bugs.
  • Maintain account security and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. Data Retention & Security

We implement 256–bit SSL encryption for all data transfers. Your professional data is stored on secure servers. You can request permanent deletion of your data at any time via your dashboard settings.

4. Third-Party Links & Cookies

Our site may contain links to external job boards or professional networks. We also use essential cookies to remember your session. We do not share your private resume data with any third-party marketing agencies.
