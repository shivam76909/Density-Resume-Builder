What Makes a Great Resume?

A strong resume should:

  • Be easy to read
  • Highlight relevant skills
  • Showcase achievements
  • Include measurable results
  • Use ATS-friendly formatting
  • Be tailored to the job

Recruiters often spend less than 10 seconds reviewing a resume initially, making structure and clarity extremely important.

Resume Example Structure

Most professional resumes include the following sections:

Contact Information

Include:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • LinkedIn Profile
  • Portfolio Website (if applicable)

Professional Summary

A professional summary provides a quick overview of your experience and qualifications.

Example:

Results-driven Data Analyst with 3+ years of experience analyzing business data, creating dashboards, and delivering actionable insights. Skilled in SQL, Power BI, Excel, and Python.

Work Experience

Employers want to see:

  • Responsibilities
  • Achievements
  • Measurable impact

Example:

Software Engineer  |  ABC Technologies  |  2023–Present

• Developed scalable web applications using React and Node.js.

• Improved application performance by 35%.

• Collaborated with cross-functional teams to launch new features.

Education

Example:

Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science

XYZ University  |  2026

Skills

Technical Skills:

  • Python
  • SQL
  • Excel
  • Java
  • Power BI

Soft Skills:

  • Communication
  • Leadership
  • Teamwork
  • Problem Solving

ATS-Friendly Resume Examples

An ATS-friendly resume should:

  • Use standard headings
  • Include relevant keywords
  • Avoid excessive graphics
  • Use clear formatting
  • Focus on achievements

Examples that follow ATS guidelines generally perform better during online applications.

Resume Writing Tips from Successful Examples

Focus on Achievements

❌ Responsible for customer support.

✔ Resolved 100+ customer inquiries weekly while maintaining a 96% satisfaction rating.

Use Numbers

Examples:

  • ✔ Increased sales by 25%
  • ✔ Reduced costs by 20%
  • ✔ Managed a team of 12 employees

Tailor Your Resume

Customize each resume according to the job description.

Keep It Relevant

Include only information that supports your application.
